Collections & Finance
The Working-Capital Win
Get paid faster. Investor-grade receivables in seconds.
The Collections Engine puts Vyara at the front of every contractor's payment queue through systematic, automated follow-up — and gives leadership clean ageing numbers on demand.
💰
Collections Engine HERO FEATURE
Automated WhatsApp Dunning + Ageing
  • Automated WhatsApp dunning via AiSensy: pre-due → overdue → escalation on a configurable cadence
  • Ageing buckets: Current / 1–30 / 31–60 / 60+ days — always live
  • Promise-to-pay (PTP) logging with follow-up date
  • Receipt recording — closes dunning, marks invoice paid
  • Per-customer account view with full payment history
  • Credit limit monitoring — flag customers exceeding limit
  • Every WhatsApp sent, every PTP, every receipt logged on timeline
  • States: due → pre-due reminded → overdue → dunning → PTP → paid / written-off
How it works in practice
Invoice synced from Tally → 3 days before due: "Your payment of ₹4.2L is due Thursday" WhatsApp auto-sent → Due date passes → Overdue WhatsApp escalates → Task created for sales engineer to call → Customer commits to payment date → PTP logged → Payment received: receipt recorded, invoice closed. Every step on the invoice timeline. No chasing spreadsheets.
